Our Inspiration

ASCII art has a long and fascinating history, tracing back to the early days of computing when graphical displays were limited. We wanted to bring this unique art form to life by merging it with modern technology, creating a live video feed that transforms into ASCII art in real-time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to blend art and technology, providing a platform where people can experience the beauty of ASCII art in a new, dynamic way. We aim to inspire creativity and showcase the potential of combining traditional art forms with digital innovation.
